== Band and genre ==
Salsaringer is a Kahlgrund-based band, which represents self written music and song texts from the Kahlgrund featuringTimo Wiesmann (Schweinheim) as the singer, Martin Brückner (Geiselbach) as the bass player, Josef Bayer (Schöllkrippen) as the guitar player and Helmut Dedio (Omersbach) as the drummer.
