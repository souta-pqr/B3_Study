The T58 had begun bench testing in 1955 and by 1958 had already been used in helicopters and de Havilland were able to test their first engines in a Westland Whirlwind and Wasp helicopters in August 1959 and March 1960 respectively.A free-turbine turboshaft, it was used in helicopters such as the Westland Sea King and Westland Whirlwind.
