=== Hiss Case ===
During the Hiss Case, Rosenwald was an attorney first in 1948 with Oseas, Pepper & Segal and by 1950 with Beer, Richards, Lane and Haller (also known as Oseas, Pepper & Siegel with offices in Washington and on Liberty Street, New York) (from 1949 to 1957 called "Beer, Richards, Lane, Haller & Buttenwieser").
