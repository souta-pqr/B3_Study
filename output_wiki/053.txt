Chenar Bagali (Persian: چناربگالي, also Romanized as Chenār Bagālī, Chenār Bāghaleh, Chenār Bāgheleh, Chenār Begālī, and Chinār Bāghle; also known as Robāghaleh) is a village in Koregah-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Khorramabad County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 222, in 50 families.
