Aksakovo (Russian: Аксаково) is a rural locality (a village) in Denisovskoye Rural Settlement, Gorokhovetsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. The population was 5 as of 2010.


== Geography ==
Aksakovo is located on the Kurzha River, 25 km (16 mi) southwest of Gorokhovets (the district's administrative centre) by road. Yeskino is the nearest rural locality.
