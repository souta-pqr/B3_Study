Franz Roggow (born 14 July 2002) is a German footballer who plays as a midfielder for Borussia Dortmund II.


== Early life ==
Roggow joined the youth academy of German side FC Energie Cottbus at the age of twelve. He attended Julius-Leber-Schule in Germany.


== Career ==
Roggow started his career with German side FC St. Pauli. He was regarded as one of the club's most promising prospects. In 2023, he signed for German side Borussia Dortmund II. On 5 August 2023, he debuted for the club during a 0–0 draw with SC Preußen Münster. On 17 February 2024, he scored his first two goals for the club during a 5–2 win over SC Verl.


== Style of play ==
Roggow mainly operates as a midfielder. He is left-footed.


== Personal life ==
Roggow is a native of Bad Freienwalde, Germany.
